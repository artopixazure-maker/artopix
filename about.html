We are ARTOPIX . We create impactful and creative print designs.

We are a team of creative designers and visual artists dedicated to developing innovative print designs.

We design prints for all areas of women’s apparel—such as garments, scarves, juniors, contemporary wear, sleepwear, swimwear, home décor, and bags.

We sell our designs to various fashion brands and fabric manufacturers around the world.

Our collection is created by a team of highly experienced and exceptional designers, many of whom have been working with us for years.The consistent quality of our design work is a direct reflection of the strong spirit and unity within our team.

While we base our collections on market trends for commercial appeal, it is our bold creative instinct that delivers unexpected designs—highly sellable and uniquely inspiring for our clients.

For inspiration, we draw from runways, fashion shows, trending markets, art galleries, vintage arts, cinema, street fashion, fairs, and more to craft a unique structure each season.

Our designers work both manually—through hand painting—and digitally, to create distinctive works. They use Photoshop, Illustrator, and Corel DRAW. We showcase our collection by mail and send PPT or PDF files upon client request.
